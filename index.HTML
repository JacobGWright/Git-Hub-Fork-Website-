What is Technical Writing?

Technical Writing is any writing that occurs in a professional setting. It is both an art and a science, relevant to any field, that develops the best methods of delivering information. It is important, especially in today's environment of mass communication, that workplace writing is done to a high standard that implements the following characteristics:

  • Accuracy
  • Clarity
  • Conciseness
  • Readability/Usability
  • Correctness

Successful technical writing will result in an informative document that guides any reader to the correct conclusions. The document must be designed and written in a way that captivates its audience, otherwise the reader may disregard most, if not all, of the contained information.

It is clear to see that technical writing includes not only writing proposals and memorandums, but also extends to a wider range of communication. This includes writing code, and designing infographics.
